What the Data Tells You

If you want US PE exposure
Freshfields or Clifford Chance. Both are aggressively growing US PE practices. Freshfields' record 2025 revenues prove the strategy is working. CC's January 2026 PE partner hire signals the same intent.
If you want the best UK M&A work
Slaughter and May. Despite being the smallest, they're instructed on a disproportionate share of FTSE 100 transactions. Trainees get early responsibility. The "best friend" model means you still get cross-border exposure.
If you want capital markets depth
Linklaters or A&O Shearman. Both are elite in debt and equity capital markets in Europe. Linklaters' emerging markets practice (Egypt bond, LatAm DCM) adds a dimension others don't match.
