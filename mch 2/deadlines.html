Application Tips

Apply early, not on the deadline
Magic Circle firms review on a rolling basis. Applications submitted 4–6 weeks before the deadline get more partner time than those on the final day. Set calendar reminders 6 weeks before each deadline.
Vacation scheme = the real route in
At most Magic Circle firms, 60–80% of training contract offers go to vacation scheme candidates. If you can, prioritise vac scheme applications. Direct TC applications are more competitive without scheme experience.
Research is your edge
Interviewers expect you to know recent deals, the firm's strategic direction, and what makes it genuinely different from its peers. Use the Firm Profiles and Compare pages on this site as your research foundation.
